About me

Hi, I'm Sheniva Nehalen Allen James, senior at Adventist Bilingual school in French Harbour with a passion for learning and growth. As a student, I'm constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to develop my skills and knowledge. Whether it's through participating in activities like Oral presentations, volunteering in my community, or taking on leadership roles in school projects, I'm always looking for ways to make a positive impact. My goal is to attend The university in Argentina which is PLATA and pursue a degree in Communication, where I hope to combine my interests in Broadcast Technicians with my passion for doing oral presentations. Ultimately, I'm excited to connect with like-minded individuals and learn from their experiences, while also sharing my own thoughts and perspectives.

Habilities

As a high school student with a passion for oral presentations, I have developed a range of skills that enable me to effectively communicate my ideas and engage my audience. One of my greatest strengths is my ability to think on my feet and respond confidently to unexpected questions or challenges. I am also highly adept at crafting compelling narratives and structuring my presentations in a clear and logical manner, making it easy for my audience to follow along and stay engaged. Furthermore, I have a keen sense of audience awareness, which allows me to tailor my tone, language, and body language to suit the specific needs and preferences of my audience. For instance, I know how to adjust my pace and volume to accommodate different learning styles and attention spans. Additionally, I am skilled at using visual aids such as slides, videos, and props to enhance my presentations and make them more interactive and memorable. My passion for oral presentations is evident in my enthusiasm and energy, which is infectious and helps to captivate my audience. Overall, I believe that my skills and experience make me a confident and effective oral presenter, and I am excited to continue developing and refining my abilities in this area.
